How it works

Three steps to your first memory

01

Connect

Share a link with your partner. Both of you sync your wearable and your heartbeats appear in real time.

02

Capture

Upload a photo from a moment you shared. ISOLA finds both heartbeats from that exact time and brings the memory to life.

03

Remember

Your memory now has a pulse. Share it, save it, or just watch two hearts beat together over a sunset.
